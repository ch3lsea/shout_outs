Welcome to Shout Outs!

This is a webpage designed by a student at Prime Digital Academy. The purpose of this site is to display "shout outs" created by fellow "Primemates".

What are shout outs?

Shout outs are a way of saying thank you to others in the program or to instructors for a job well done. Anyone can create a shout out to say thanks to any other person in Prime for any task, big or small.
